With the real world in ruins,
Arantor is a virtual refuge

A virtual game world set in fantasy that anyone can play, but not everyone is equal. Jane has played for years but by no fault of her own is hamstrung compared to other players.

A glitch changes everything for Jane, but her new found power comes with a price - and plenty of guilds and players seek to use her for their own gain.

Then Jane begins a dangerous quest...

With her friends and unexpected companions...

They journey to the far ends of Arantor...

...where even the rules of the game can change.
